Laurel's Black Box Investigation

DNA Analysis Project

Introduction/Background

We did an investigation at ESUMS about DNA Analysis. Our goal was to use see which other 9th grades we were most related to using DNA extraction, PCR amplification, and Gel Elctrophoresis.

Materials List

  • Micropipettor (Pipette Tips)
  • Tubes
  • Flat-ended Toothpick
  • X-Tract™️ buffer.
  • D1S80 Primer Mix
  • 2X EZ PCR Master MixTM
  • Student DNA sample
  • miniPCR® thermal cycler
  • BanditTM circuit controller
  • Light Strip
  • Electrodams
  • Electrode Cable

Whar We Did

First we started with DNA extraction, where we got at least one copy of each of our DNA's. Next we added in the regeants, D1S80 Primer Mix and 2X EZ PCR Master Mix™️, into our DNA. Our next step was PCR Amplification, where we made billions of copies of our DNA of interest, in our experiment was a VNTR. Then we moved onto Gel Electrophoresis. In gel electrophoresis, electricity is conducted through the agarose gel and the liquid buffer that surrounds it to seperate molecules. We then measured the DNA copies on Adobe Photoshop based on length to discover who we matched with. The in teams, we created a documentary consisting of initial and final interviews, b-roll of the project, and a stop motion animation.
